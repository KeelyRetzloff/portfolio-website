Project Background

This project was a to-go menu design for a Cajun Seafood Restaurant and Bar. The idea was to use the current branding to create a pocket-sized to-go menu that could be folded and put away easily.

Goals and Objectives

Had to be pocket-sized and foldable, had to be clear, readable and fit in with the brand.

Creative strategy

This menu was essentially a smaller version of the full menu with just enough spacing to be readable in pamphlet form. After some research on competitor menus, it seemed liked a good idea to add a map and create the folding line at it to maximize the menu usefullness. Pamphlet was printed on card stock paper.
